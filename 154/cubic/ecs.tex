	Consider the elliptic curve $E\colon y^2=x^3+1$ with marked point $\OO$ at infinity. Consider the points $P\coloneqq(-1,0)$ and $Q\coloneqq(0,1)$ on $E$.
	\begin{listalph}
		\item Compute $P+Q$.
		\item Compute $2Q$.
		\item Compute $P+2Q$.
	\end{listalph}
\end{prob}
